Getting Started

OnoAir is your go-to platform for booking flights and managing flight operations. Whether you're a traveler or an administrator, this guide will help you make the most of our application.

For Users

Booking a Flight

  1. Navigate to the User - Book a Flight section from the main screen.
  2. Use the search filters to select your Origin and Destination.
  3. Click the Search button to view available flights.
  4. Review the list of flights and click Book on your preferred flight.
  5. Enter the number of passengers and provide passenger details.
  6. Click Save Booking to confirm your reservation.

Managing Your Bookings

  1. Go to the User - Manage Bookings section from the main screen.
  2. View a list of your current and past bookings.
  3. Click on a booking to see more details.

For Admins

Managing Flights

  1. Access the Admin - Manage Flights section from the main screen.
  2. View the list of all scheduled flights.
  3. To add a new flight, click on the Add New Flight button.
  4. Fill out the flight details, including Flight Number, Origin, Destination, Departure and Arrival times, and Seats Available.
  5. Click Save Flight to add the flight to the schedule.

Managing Destinations

  1. Go to the Admin - Manage Destinations section from the main screen.
  2. Review the list of all destinations.
  3. To add a new destination, click on the Add New Destination button.
  4. Provide the Destination Code, Destination Name, Airport Name, Airport Website URL, Email Address, and Image URL.
  5. Click Save Destination to add it to the system.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a flight?

Navigate to the User - Book a Flight section, select your origin and destination, and click Search.

Can I manage my bookings?

Yes, you can view your bookings in the User - Manage Bookings section.

As an admin, how do I add a new flight?

Go to the Admin - Manage Flights section and click on Add New Flight.
